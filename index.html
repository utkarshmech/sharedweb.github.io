The Stochastic Simulation:

**Uncertain parameters: **Laser power, Material properties (density of powder) and speed of traverse, initial porosity

Target properties: Depth of melt zone, Maximum temperature, Width of melt zone final.

Major Methods :

Monte Carlo simulation Polynomial Chaos Methods
More iterations for similar accuracy Relative high accuracy and more efficient on computation
Large no. of simulation required Very few iterations required
Proof of curse of dimensionality Most suitable for for dimension of 3-50 random variables

Formulation overview:

The base model which will be iterated over for the stochastic simulations.

  • The base model will only comprise of the non-moving laser beam with varying exposure time.
  • Simplistic mesh will be taken for faster simulations.
  • Slab size as mentioned in previous simulations.

Varying Input Parameters:

  • Initial Porosity - Gaussian, log-Normal
  • Laser Intensity/power-
    • variation in peak intensity and
    • variation in type of guassian
  • Velocity or exposure Time.
  • Laser area

Output parameters to be considered

  • Depth of the melt-zone
  • Diameter of melt-zone
  • Average percent porosity
  • Max temperature.
